Auditions for the Fall 25’ show, Peace in Our Time are Friday, September 12th, with callbacks Saturday, Sept 13th!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

Voice Audition: In the show there is a character known as voice who is a radio announcer. For this role all your lines would be recorded and played during the show, so you would not be required to be on stage. If you are interested in ONLY auditioning for voice please record a voice recording of you rehearsing a monologue of your choice and EMAIL it to ALL of prod-staff. All of their emails can be found in the slideshow. If you are interested in auditioning for voice AND other characters just make sure you mention it at your audition.
